Crypt Sermon

Epic doom band formed in 2013 by bassist and vocalist Brooks Wilson, guitarists Steve Jansson and James Lipczynski and drummer Enrique Sagarnaga. In 2015 the debut album Out of the Garden was released, on which the band had worked for about a year and a half. In 2019, they released The Ruins of Fading Light.
